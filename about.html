ABOUT ME

Hello! I’m Tenzin, a passionate UI/UX designer and frontend developer. With a background in physics, I bring a unique analytical perspective to the creative world of design. My journey has been fueled by a love for problem-solving and creating intuitive user experiences.

Educational Background

I hold a Master’s degree in Physics, where I developed strong analytical skills and a deep understanding of complex systems. This foundation has not only enhanced my critical thinking but also informs my design approach, allowing me to create data-driven, user-centered solutions.

Design Philosophy

I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics; it’s about solving real problems and enhancing user experiences. My design philosophy centers around empathy, usability, and simplicity. I strive to create interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also accessible and easy to navigate.

Skills

  • UI/UX Design: Proficient in tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Sketch. I specialize in wireframing, prototyping, and conducting user research.
  • Frontend Development: Skilled in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a focus on creating responsive and interactive web applications.
  • Collaboration: Experienced in working with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless integration of design and development.
