ABOUT ME

Hello! I’m a frontend developer with a unique blend of technical expertise and creative flair. With a master’s degree in physics, I bring a strong analytical mindset to my work, allowing me to approach problems with precision and innovative solutions. My journey into tech has led me to specialize in UI and UX design, where I’m passionate about creating intuitive and visually stunning user experiences. Throughout my career, I’ve developed a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as frameworks like React and Vue.js. My background in physics enhances my ability to think critically and tackle complex challenges, ensuring that my designs are not only beautiful but also functional and user-centered.

I thrive on collaboration and am always eager to learn from others, staying updated with the latest industry trends and best practices. When I’m not coding or designing, you can find me exploring the intersections of science and technology, or enjoying a good hike. Let’s connect and create something amazing together!
